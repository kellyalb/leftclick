Privacy Policy

Information about our Organization and Web site

Providing Visitors with Anonymous Access

You can access our Web sites home page and browse our sites without disclosing your personal data.

The services and links of our Web sites

Our Web sites enable you to communicate with other visitors or to post information to be accessed by others. When you do so, other visitors may collect your data.

Third party web service providers

Data may be collected about your visits in a non-personally identifiable way by Google Analytics, a third party web service provider. No other third party web service provider may collect information about your visits to the sites covered by this privacy policy.

We collect the personal data that you may volunteer while using our services. We do not collect information about our visitors from other sources, such as public records or bodies, or private organizations.

We may collect the following personal data about you to assist us in providing services, maintaining contracts, and in customer administration:

  • Contact information: Name, Address, E-mail address, Phone/Fax number.
  • Records of your website usage.
  • Computer settings, file information, serial numbers, passwords (if required).
  • Records of your use of our services
  • Records of your payment details (e.g. How you paid).
We may collect the following personal data about you for Marketing Purposes: Name, E-mail address.

If we wish to use your personal data for a new purpose, we offer you the means to consent to this new purpose by emailing us at privacy@leftclick.us. This privacy policy will be updated to reflect any new uses of your personal data.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. We make reasonable efforts to ensure that a parent has authorized the collection of the child's personal data.

Confidentiality / Security

We have implemented security policies, rules and technical measures to protect the personal data that we have under our control from:

All our employees and data processors, who have access to, and are associate with the processing of personal data, are obliged to respect the confidentiality of our visitors' personal data.

We do not disclose your personal data to our subsidiaries or other organizations.

We ensure that your personal data will not be disclosed to State institutions and authorities except if required by law or other regulation.

Upon request, which you can indicate by sending an email ( privacy@leftclick.us ), we will provide you with a readable copy of the personal data which we keep about you, - although we may before require proof of your identity -. We will provide the information without any charge. We allow you to challenge the data that we hold about you and, where appropriate, you may have the data erased, rectified or amended or completed.

We reserve the right to refuse to provide our visitors with a copy of their personal data, but will give reasons for our refusal. We do, however, allow you to challenge our decision to refuse to provide you with a copy of your personal data.
